Local publishing

In some situation you may want to publish shirates-core repository locally. For example you can not access to remote shirates-core repository, or you are developing and debugging new feature of shirates-core. You can build shirates-core project and publish locally, and refer to it.

  1. Open shirates-core project, double-click publishToLocalRepository in Gradle pane.

  1. Open your project, refer to build/repository in shirates-core project.

Given that shirates-core project is cloned under $userHome/github.

val userHome = System.getProperty("user.home")

repositories {
    mavenCentral()

    maven(url = "file:/$userHome/github/shirates-core/build/repository")
}
  1. Click reload in Gradle pane.
